About

Researcher, engineer,
and curious human.

I'm a postgraduate student at Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW), specialising in Artificial Intelligence as part of the MTech (CSE–AI) programme. My undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering (Honors) was from MITS, Gwalior, completed in 2024.

I care deeply about applying data science and machine learning to problems that matter — from healthcare diagnostics to urban transportation. My research is shaped by the belief that the most powerful algorithms are the ones that improve people's daily lives.

Outside of research, I find balance through writing, photography, music, and reading — a reminder that technology is most meaningful when it stays rooted in human experience.
